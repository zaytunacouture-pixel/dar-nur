# Dar Nūr — Design Brief (ground truth for audit)

Single-page static site (index.html, ~170KB, no framework). Premium natural-products
brand: artisanal honeys, plant oils, herbal capsules, powders/seeds, plus modest
clothing (abayas, qamis) and natural mists. Orders flow exclusively through WhatsApp.
Delivery in Île-de-France (hand-delivered). Founder: Youcef.

## Design tokens (CSS :root)
- --green:#0d1f16 (near-black forest green, primary brand)
- --green-soft:#16301f
- --gold:#c8a84b ; --gold-light:#dcc079 (metallic gold accents)
- --cream:#f4efe4 ; --offwhite:#faf8f3 (warm paper backgrounds)
- --muted:#6b6256 (warm grey-brown text)
- --line:rgba(200,168,75,.28) (gold hairline borders)

## Typography
- Headings: 'Cinzel' (Roman inscriptional serif), weight 600, letter-spacing .04–.34em,
  frequently UPPERCASE. Used for h1/h2/h3, brand mark, eyebrows, nav, trust items, CTAs.
- Accent/quote: 'Cormorant Garamond' italic — story blockquote, hero subtitle, Arabic.
- Body: 'Lora' serif.
- Arabic calligraphy (دار النور) appears in hero, story, footer.
- Hero h1 uses clamp(2.1rem,5.5vw,3.7rem). Heavy reliance on three serif families;
  NO sans-serif anywhere. Everything is serif + gold + uppercase letterspacing.

## Page structure (top → bottom)
1. header: sticky nav, brand lockup (logo + "DAR NŪR" + Arabic), links BOUTIQUE /
   BIEN-ÊTRE / MODE (dropdowns) / NOTRE HISTOIRE / QUI SOMMES-NOUS / FAQ / AVIS. Burger on mobile.
2. hero: dark-green radial gradient (#1a3a26→--green), faint Islamic 8-point-star +
   circle SVG motif tiled at opacity .13/.5. Centered: logo image in gold-bordered
   rounded box (180px), "DAR NŪR" Cinzel gold, Arabic subtitle, eyebrow "LA MAISON DE
   LA LUMIÈRE", h1 "LES TRÉSORS DE LA NATURE, LIVRÉS AVEC CONFIANCE", italic gold sub
   "Sélectionnés avec soin — si Dieu le veut". NO primary CTA button in hero.
3. trust-bar: white strip, 4 items w/ gold icons — LIVRAISON OFFERTE EN ÎLE-DE-FRANCE /
   PAIEMENT SÉCURISÉ / RÉPONSE WHATSAPP EN MOINS D'UNE HEURE / PRODUITS SÉLECTIONNÉS AVEC
   SOIN. Cinzel .72rem uppercase. On mobile collapses to 1 column, left-aligned.
4. story: cream bg, big gold Arabic, italic Cormorant blockquote (founder's voice,
   2 paragraphs), signoff "— YOUCEF, FONDATEUR DE DAR NŪR".
5. boutique: section-head (eyebrow "NOTRE COLLECTION" / h2 "La Boutique" / italic sub),
   filter pills (TOUS, MIELS, GÉLULES, POUDRES & GRAINES, HUILES, BRUMES, QAMIS, ABAYAS),
   responsive product grid. Each card: category eyebrow, product name, "dès 9,99",
   "COMMANDER SUR WHATSAPP" button. ~60+ products. No cart, no prices-with-currency-symbol
   consistency, no add-to-cart — every CTA opens WhatsApp.
6. about ("QUI SOMMES-NOUS"): offwhite, 3 points Sélection / Conseil / Confiance.
7. customer-info ("COMMANDE & LIVRAISON").
8. faq: 7 Q&A (commander, délais 2-3j, paiement Revolut+espèces, contact WhatsApp, suivi,
   hors IDF, problème).
9. reviews ("AVIS"): testimonials (e.g. "— Naïma", 5/5 stars).
10. pay: payment reassurance.
11. footer: dark (#0a160f), brand lockup, columns, trust row.
Product detail view (pp-hero / engage / family) is rendered client-side via JS templates.

## Spacing state (after this session's aggressive compression)
Vertical padding was deliberately crushed to get the boutique above the fold fast:
- section{padding:15px 28px} (desktop), {12px 22px} (≤768px)
- .hero{padding:15px 28px 17px} (desktop), {20px 22px 25px} (≤768px)
- .trust-bar{padding:4px 28px}
- .story{padding:6px 28px}, blockquote margins 6px, line-height 1.5
- .section-head{margin-bottom:16px}
The Collections hub (5 category cards between story and boutique) was REMOVED this session.
Risk: spacing may now read as cramped / under-breathing rather than premium-calm.

## Imagery
- Logo: gold Arabic calligraphy "نور / Dar Nūr" with crescent + star, on dark.
- Product photos: honey jars w/ wood dippers, abaya flat-lays/model shots (dar-nur-*.jpg),
  webp powder/spray shots. Mixed sources, mixed framing, not a unified shot system.
- Background: SVG Islamic geometric star motif (decorative, low opacity).

## Conversion model
WhatsApp-only. No checkout, no cart, no account. CTA "COMMANDER SUR WHATSAPP" repeated on
every product card and as floating mobile bar (.mbar). Trust signals: free delivery (IDF),
secure payment, <1h WhatsApp reply, hand delivery in 2-3 days, Revolut/cash.

## Known finish issues to weigh
- google-site-verification meta still literally "REMPLACER_PAR_VOTRE_VALEUR_EXISTANTE".
- Three competing serif families + ubiquitous uppercase letterspacing → ornate, not minimal.
- Gold-on-dark-green is rich but high-contrast-ornate vs Apple's neutral restraint.
- Product card density + repeated green "COMMANDER SUR WHATSAPP" buttons.
- Imagery not normalized (varied backgrounds, crops, lighting).

## Reference: Apple (apple.com) design language
Massive negative space; ruthless minimalism; SF Pro sans-serif system; few weights; huge
hero product shots on pure white/black; one idea per viewport; generous whitespace as luxury
signal; restrained palette (white/black/grey + product color); precise optical alignment;
consistent shot system (studio, shadowless or controlled); short declarative copy; clear
primary CTA ("Buy"/"Learn more") pair; flawless responsive parity; micro-interaction polish.
